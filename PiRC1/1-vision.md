# 1. Vision: ZYN Launchpad — Utility-First Marketplace Token

**ZYN Launchpad** introduces a marketplace-native token launch framework built on the Pi ecosystem. Unlike generic launchpads, ZYN is designed from the ground up for **commerce-first utility** — where the token's value is anchored in real transactions, merchant services, and cross-app rewards.

## 1.1 Core Principles

- **Immediate Utility**: ZYN tokens are usable from TGE for marketplace discounts, seller fees, and cross-app purchases — no speculative waiting period.
- **Merchant Alignment**: Merchants must stake ZYN proportional to their sales volume, creating natural buy pressure and aligning incentives.
- **Community Rewards**: Active marketplace participants (buyers, reviewers, referrers) earn ZYN through engagement — not just staking.
- **Sustainable Deflation**: A 0.5% burn on every ZYN transaction ensures the token supply decreases as marketplace activity increases.
- **Cross-App Portability**: ZYN is not locked to ZynMart — any Pi app can integrate ZYN as a payment or reward mechanism.

## 1.2 Why ZYN Improves on PiRC1

PiRC1 introduced the concept of PiPower and staking-based participation. While innovative, it treats all apps equally and doesn't account for the unique dynamics of marketplace ecosystems where:

1. **Merchants are both producers and consumers** of token utility
2. **Transaction volume** is a more reliable engagement signal than generic app usage
3. **Liquidity needs** are different for commerce (frequent small swaps) vs. speculation
4. **Cross-app value transfer** is essential for ecosystem growth

ZYN addresses each of these gaps while preserving PiRC1's core strengths: fair access, predictable liquidity, and anti-rug-pull protections.
