# 7. Loyalty & Rewards System

## 7.1 ZYN Cashback

Users who pay with ZYN on ZynMart receive cashback in ZYN:

| User Tier | Cashback Rate | Qualification |
|---|---|---|
| **Basic** | 2% | Any ZYN payment |
| **Silver Staker** | 5% | Stake ≥ 200 ZYN |
| **Gold Staker** | 8% | Stake ≥ 1,000 ZYN |
| **Platinum Staker** | 12% | Stake ≥ 5,000 ZYN |

Cashback is distributed as locked ZYN with 30-day linear release, preventing immediate sell pressure.

## 7.2 Spin Wheel Rewards

The Spin Wheel distributes ZYN rewards daily:

- **Free Spin**: 1-10 ZYN (locked, 7-day release)
- **Ad Spin**: 5-50 ZYN (locked, 14-day release)
- **Premium Spin**: 10-100 ZYN (locked, 30-day release)

Spin rewards are funded from the **Ecosystem Development** allocation (10% of total supply).

## 7.3 Promo Code System

Merchants and the ZynMart team can create promo codes that mint ZYN rewards:

- **Merchant Promo**: Merchant-funded, drives traffic to their store
- **Platform Promo**: ZynMart-funded, drives platform engagement
- **Partner Promo**: Cross-app partner-funded, drives ecosystem growth

All promo codes have:
- Expiration date
- Usage cap
- Lock period (prevents immediate selling)
- On-chain verification (prevents fraud)

## 7.4 Referral Program

Users who refer new merchants or buyers earn ZYN:

| Referral Type | Reward | Condition |
|---|---|---|
| **Buyer Referral** | 10 ZYN | Referred user completes first purchase |
| **Merchant Referral** | 100 ZYN | Referred merchant completes first 10 sales |
| **App Referral** | 500 ZYN | Referred Pi app integrates ZYN payments |

Referral rewards are locked for 30 days.

## 7.5 Review Incentives

Users earn ZYN for writing verified product reviews:

- **Text Review**: 2 ZYN
- **Photo Review**: 5 ZYN
- **Video Review**: 10 ZYN

Anti-abuse measures:
- Maximum 5 reviews per user per day
- Must have purchased the product
- AI + community moderation for fake review detection
- Review farming results in permanent ban from rewards
